1. SMS Program Overview

Equisharer offers an SMS messaging program to provide you with:

  • Updates about your business inquiries and partnership opportunities
  • Business growth tips and marketing insights
  • Important announcements about our services
  • Promotional offers and special opportunities
  • Appointment reminders and follow-up communications

2. Consent and Opt-In

By providing your mobile phone number and checking the SMS opt-in box on our forms, you:

  • Expressly consent to receive SMS messages from Equisharer
  • Confirm that you are the owner or authorized user of the mobile phone number provided
  • Agree to receive messages at the number provided, even if it's on a Do Not Call list
  • Understand that consent is not required to purchase our services
  • Acknowledge that message and data rates may apply

3. Message Frequency and Content

Message Frequency:

  • Varies based on your inquiry and engagement level
  • Typically 2-4 messages per month for general updates
  • May increase during active partnership discussions
  • Promotional messages limited to 1-2 per month

Message content may include business updates, growth tips, appointment reminders, promotional offers, and other information relevant to our partnership services.

4. Opt-Out Instructions

How to Stop Messages:

  • Reply STOP to any SMS message to unsubscribe immediately
  • Reply UNSUBSCRIBE as an alternative opt-out method
  • Contact us directly at 925-268-0094 to opt-out
  • Email us at equisharerllc@gmail.com with your opt-out request

Once you opt-out, you will receive a confirmation message, and no further messages will be sent to your number unless you opt-in again.
